Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Overview

This integration is an internal tool used solely by the workspace owner and authorized team members. It is not a public-facing service and does not collect data from the general public.

2. Data We Access

This integration accesses Notion workspace data (pages, databases, tasks) and Slack workspace data (messages, channels) only as necessary to perform its intended functions.

No personal data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

This integration does not store user data on external servers. All data remains within the connected Notion and Slack workspaces. Temporary processing may occur in memory but is not persisted.

4. Data Security

Access tokens and credentials are stored securely and never exposed publicly. Only authorized workspace members may access this integration.

5. Your Rights

As this integration operates within your own workspace, you retain full control of your data. You may revoke access at any time through your Notion or Slack settings.

6. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the workspace administrator directly.
